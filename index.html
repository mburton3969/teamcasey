Our Mission

We put our utmost efforts into restoring a self-supporting livelihood to those people who have been stricken with hardship.

Our Programs

Charity programs provide life-transforming experiences, especially for people who are recovering from trauma.

Help & Support

Our volunteers and team members provide extensive help and support to those experiencing health problems.

Our Services

Charity cuts poverty off at its roots. We provide tools and services for sustainable change to the people most vulnerable to hunger, violence, and disease.

Child Protective Services

The primary goal of these services is to protect children from abuse, neglect, or dependency, as well as to serve their families.

Social Support Programs

We offer a variety of social support programs that are aimed at social assistance for every society member.

Fundraising Assistance

Yearly we raise more than $50M for charity needs and we will be glad to assist you in any fundraising matter.

Event Organizing and Management

We can help you organize a charitable or fundraising event to help attract attention to a certain society problem.
